What to Do If You Have a Problem Online

How to Save Evidence
  • Don't delete abusive messages
  • Take screenshots of conversations
  • Write down the date and time
Who to Tell
  • Your parents or guardian
  • Your teacher or school counselor
  • Child protection police
When to Ask for Help
  • If you receive scary messages
  • If someone asks for personal information
  • If you see inappropriate content
